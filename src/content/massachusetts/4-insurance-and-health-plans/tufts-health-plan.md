## Overview

Tufts Health Plan (part of Point32Health after merger with Harvard Pilgrim) is a major Massachusetts health insurer offering Medicare Advantage, MassHealth, and commercial plans. With extensive networks across MA, Tufts represents significant referral opportunities for home care agencies.

**Plans:** Tufts Medicare Preferred (Medicare Advantage), Tufts Health Together (MassHealth), Commercial plans

**Provider portal:** point32health.org/provider

## Who Sends Referrals

- Care coordinators for Medicare Advantage and MassHealth members
- Network PCPs and specialists
- Hospital discharge planners
- Members self-referring

## Who Can Receive Referrals

- Medicare-certified home health (covered benefits)
- Private-pay home care (supplemental services)
- Adult day health, hospice, DME

## How It Works

1. **Enroll through Point32Health provider portal**
2. **Complete credentialing** for desired plan types
3. **Receive referrals** from care coordinators and network providers
4. **Bill covered services** per contract; offer private-pay supplements

## Cost and Lead Quality

**Cost:** FREE enrollment; contracted rates

**Lead Quality:** High—Mix of Medicare Advantage (well-insured) and MassHealth (state-funded) members

## Best Practices

1. **Enroll in multiple plan types** (Medicare Advantage + MassHealth) for broader reach
2. **Build care coordinator relationships**
3. **Position private-pay supplements** for members needing additional hours
4. **Participate in Point32Health quality initiatives**
