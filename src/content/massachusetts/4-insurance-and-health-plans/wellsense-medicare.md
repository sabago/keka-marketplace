## Overview

WellSense Medicare is a **rapidly growing Medicare Advantage plan** in Massachusetts, serving 15,000+ Medicare beneficiaries statewide. As the Medicare division of WellSense Health Plan (formerly Boston Medical Center HealthNet Plan), WellSense Medicare brings deep experience serving vulnerable populations and offers comprehensive Medicare Advantage coverage with strong care coordination and supplemental benefits.

**Why WellSense Medicare matters for home care agencies:**
- **15,000+ Medicare members:** Fast-growing Medicare Advantage plan
- **Statewide coverage:** All Massachusetts counties (unlike regional plans)
- **Mission-driven:** Non-profit focused on vulnerable populations, health equity
- **Care coordination:** 40+ care coordinators actively managing home care referrals
- **Supplemental benefits:** Home care services included in most plans (personal care, homemaker, respite)
- **Growth trajectory:** Expanding rapidly (20%+ annual member growth)
- **Parent organization:** WellSense Health Plan has 600,000+ members total (Medicare + MassHealth + Commercial)
- **Hospital partnerships:** Strong relationships with safety-net hospitals (Boston Medical Center, Cambridge Health Alliance, Tufts Medical Center)
- **Cultural competency focus:** Multilingual care managers, emphasis on serving diverse populations

**Investment:** FREE credentialing (no enrollment fees)

**Timeline:** 8-12 weeks for provider approval

## How Massachusetts Home Care Agencies Can Receive Referrals from WellSense Medicare

**WellSense Medicare creates FIVE referral pathways:**

### **Pathway 1: Care Coordinator Direct Referrals (PRIMARY - 75%)**

**How it works:**
- WellSense's 40+ care coordinators conduct health risk assessments
- Care coordinator identifies member needing home care (ADL decline, post-hospitalization, chronic disease support, caregiver burnout, social isolation)
- Care coordinator searches provider directory for agencies with needed capabilities (language, cultural competency, specialty services)
- Care coordinator calls 2-3 agencies with member information
- Your agency responds within 4-24 hours
- Complete in-home assessment within 48-72 hours (24 hours for hospital discharges)
- Submit culturally appropriate care plan to care coordinator
- Care coordinator reviews, authorizes services (60-90 day initial authorization)
- Begin services, document, submit claims

**Conversion rate:** 85-92% (care coordinator endorsement + member trust)

**Volume potential:** Established agencies receive 2-6 care coordinator referrals monthly

**Pro tip:** WellSense care coordinators prioritize cultural competency. Emphasize multilingual staff, experience serving diverse populations, cultural sensitivity training.

### **Pathway 2: Hospital/SNF Discharge Referrals (18%)**

**How it works:**
- WellSense member hospitalized (Boston Medical Center, Cambridge Health Alliance, Tufts Medical Center, Lowell General, Brockton Hospital)
- Hospital discharge planner contacts WellSense utilization management
- WellSense care coordinator coordinates discharge planning
- Care coordinator arranges home care for safe transition
- In-network agencies with needed capabilities (language, cultural competency) contacted
- First agency to respond typically wins

**Conversion rate:** 70-85%

**Volume potential:** 1-4 hospital discharge referrals monthly

**Pro tip:** Build relationships with discharge planners at safety-net hospitals (Boston Medical Center, Cambridge Health Alliance, Tufts Medical Center). These hospitals have high WellSense volume.

### **Pathway 3: Member Self-Referral (5%)**

**How it works:**
- Member finds your agency via Google, referral, community organization
- Verify WellSense Medicare coverage
- Contact WellSense Provider Services (877-957-1300) to verify benefits, authorization
- Complete assessment, submit care plan to care coordinator
- Care coordinator authorizes
- Begin care

**Conversion rate:** 85-90%

**Volume potential:** 0-2 member-initiated referrals monthly

**Pro tip:** Optimize Google Business Profile for multilingual searches ("WellSense Medicare home care," "cuidado en el hogar WellSense Medicare").

### **Pathway 4: Primary Care Physician Referrals (2%)**

**How it works:**
- Member visits PCP
- PCP identifies home care need
- PCP notifies WellSense care coordinator
- Care coordinator contacts member, then agencies
- You complete assessment, care coordinator authorizes

**Conversion rate:** 75-85%

**Volume potential:** 0-1 PCP referrals monthly

**Pro tip:** Focus on community health centers (Boston Medical Center primary care, Cambridge Health Alliance, Greater Lawrence Family Health Center, Brockton Neighborhood Health Center).

### **Pathway 5: Supplemental Benefits Referrals (<1%)**

**How it works:**
- Member inquires about supplemental home care benefits
- Directed to care coordinator or vendor
- Agencies contacted
- Complete assessment, begin services

**Conversion rate:** 65-80%

**Volume potential:** 0-1 supplemental benefit referrals monthly
