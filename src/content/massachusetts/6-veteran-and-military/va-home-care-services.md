## Overview

The Department of Veterans Affairs operates home care programs for eligible veterans in Massachusetts through:
- **VA Boston Healthcare System** (Jamaica Plain, Brockton, Worcester)
- **VA Central Western Massachusetts** (Leeds, Worcester, Springfield, Fitchburg, Greenfield, Pittsfield)

VA contracts with community agencies to provide **homemaker/home health aide services** for veterans who don't require VA-provided medical home care but need assistance with daily living.

**Opportunity:** Become VA-approved community provider to serve veterans.

## Who Sends Referrals

- **VA social workers** and care coordinators
- **VA primary care physicians**
- **VA geriatrics teams**

## Who Can Receive Referrals

- Community home care agencies contracted with VA
- Medicare-certified home health agencies (for medical services)

**Services:** Homemaker, home health aide, personal care, companion care

## How It Works

1. **Contact VA contracting office:**
   - VA Boston: (857) 364-4000
   - VA Central Western MA: (413) 584-4040

2. **Express interest in community provider program:**
   - Ask about homemaker/home health aide contracts

3. **Complete VA contracting process:**
   - Vendor registration (SAM.gov)
   - VA-specific credentialing
   - Background checks for all caregivers
   - Insurance requirements

4. **Receive veteran referrals:**
   - VA care coordinators refer eligible veterans
   - Provide authorized services per care plan

5. **Bill VA per contract terms**

## Cost and Lead Quality

**Cost:** FREE to apply; contracted rates (federal rates, similar to Medicare/MassHealth)

**Lead Quality:** Good
- **Pre-qualified:** VA has assessed veteran's eligibility and need
- **Stable payment:** Federal government backing
- **Long-term relationships:** Veterans often need ongoing care
- **Patriotic mission:** Serving those who served

## Best Practices

1. **Hire veteran caregivers:** Veterans often prefer care from fellow veterans
2. **Understand VA culture:** Military background, respect for service
3. **Navigate VA bureaucracy:** Contracting process is complex—be patient
4. **Build VA relationships:** Attend VA community partner events
5. **Track outcomes:** VA values quality metrics and veteran satisfaction
