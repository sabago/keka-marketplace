## Overview

The Home Care Alliance of Massachusetts is the **premier non-profit trade association** for the Massachusetts home care and hospice industry, representing **180+ agencies** across the state. The Alliance provides advocacy, education, networking, and a public-facing provider directory.

**Website:** thinkhomecare.org
**Provider Directory:** thinkhomecare.org/page/agencies

**Value for agencies:** Membership provides professional credibility, industry connections, policy advocacy, and visibility through the consumer-facing website.

## Who Sends Referrals

- **Consumers** searching thinkhomecare.org provider directory
- **Professional network:** Fellow members referring overflow or specialized cases
- **Policymakers and media:** Looking for credible agencies to partner with or interview

## Who Can Receive Referrals

- Member home care and hospice agencies
- All service types (non-medical, medical, hospice)

## How It Works

1. **Join as member agency:**
   - Visit thinkhomecare.org and apply for membership
   - Membership dues based on agency size

2. **Get listed in provider directory:**
   - Automatic inclusion with membership
   - Profile includes services, coverage area, contact info

3. **Receive referrals:**
   - Consumers search directory by location and service type
   - Consumers contact agencies directly

4. **Network with members:**
   - Attend Alliance conferences, webinars, regional meetings
   - Build relationships with other agencies for overflow referrals and partnerships

## Cost and Lead Quality

**Cost:** Membership dues vary by agency size (~$500-$3,000+/year typically)

**Lead Quality:** Moderate
- **Self-directed consumers:** Families searching for credible agencies
- **Professional referrals:** High-quality overflow from fellow members
- **Conversion:** 20-30% for directory leads; 50-70% for member referrals

## Best Practices

1. **Complete directory profile thoroughly**
2. **Attend Alliance events:** Conferences, webinars, regional meetings
3. **Participate in committees:** Build visibility and influence
4. **Network actively:** Establish overflow/backup partnerships with fellow members
5. **Leverage advocacy:** Alliance advocates for favorable home care policies
6. **Use member resources:** Compliance tools, HR resources, educational materials
