## Overview

**Dial 2-1-1** or call **(877) 211-6277** for Mass 211, the statewide information and referral service operated by United Way. Available **24/7** in **multiple languages**, Mass 211 connects Massachusetts residents with social services, health services, and aging resources—including home care agencies.

**Annual call volume:** Hundreds of thousands of calls, representing massive referral potential for agencies listed in Mass 211's database.

**Website:** mass211.org (searchable resource database)

## Who Sends Referrals

- **Mass 211 call center specialists** who assess caller needs and provide referrals
- **Online self-service:** Families search mass211.org database

**Who calls 211:**
- Families seeking senior care resources
- Seniors needing support services
- Social workers and case managers seeking resources for clients
- Community organizations connecting residents to services

## Who Can Receive Referrals

All social and health services:
- Home care agencies
- Home health agencies
- Food pantries, transportation, housing assistance
- Mental health services, substance use treatment
- Utility assistance, legal aid, etc.

## How It Works

1. **Family/senior calls 211** seeking help with aging-at-home challenges
2. **Specialist assesses needs:** Location, services needed, budget, urgency
3. **Specialist searches database** for home care agencies serving caller's area
4. **Specialist provides 2-3 agency names/numbers** (including yours if you're listed)
5. **Family contacts agencies directly**

**Self-service:** Families can also search mass211.org database directly

## How to Get Listed / Enrolled

**Step 1:** Contact Mass 211
- Call (877) 211-6277 and ask to be added to provider database
- Or email via mass211.org contact form

**Step 2:** Provide agency information
- Agency name, address, phone
- Services offered
- Geographic coverage
- Population served (seniors, adults with disabilities, etc.)
- Hours of operation
- Languages spoken

**Step 3:** Update periodically
- Contact Mass 211 if services, coverage, or contact info changes

## Cost and Lead Quality

**Cost:** **100% FREE** to be listed

**Lead Quality:** Good
- **Active seekers:** Families calling 211 are taking initiative
- **Diverse needs:** Mix of urgent and planning-ahead inquiries
- **Financial constraints:** Many 211 callers have limited resources (but not all)
- **Conversion:** 20-40% (lower than warm referrals; families shopping multiple agencies)

**Volume:** Depends on listing visibility and local competition

## Best Practices

1. **Ensure accurate listing:** Complete profile with all services, coverage areas, languages
2. **Update regularly:** Keep contact info current
3. **Respond quickly:** First agency to call back often wins
4. **Offer flexible payment:** Many 211 callers need sliding scale or payment plans
5. **Track source:** Ask callers "Did you find us through 211?" to measure ROI

## How This Fits Mastering Home Care

**Ingest:** Phone calls from 211-referred families

**Automation:**
- **Lead source tracking:** Tag as "Mass 211"
- **Response time monitoring:** Alert staff of 211 calls for fast follow-up
- **Conversion tracking:** Measure 211 leads → clients
