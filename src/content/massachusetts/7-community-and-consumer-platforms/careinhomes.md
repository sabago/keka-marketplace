## Overview

CareInHomes is a **lead generation and matching platform** for senior care, connecting families with home care agencies through proprietary matching algorithms. With **500,000+ unique monthly visitors**, CareInHomes provides qualified leads to agencies across the United States, including Massachusetts.

**Provider portal:** providers.careinhomes.com
**Contact:** (800) 327-7302 | Support@CareInHomes.com

**Business model:** Paid lead generation service—agencies purchase leads based on geography and service types.

## Who Sends Referrals

- **Automated matching system:** Families submit care needs → algorithm matches with agencies
- **Account managers:** CareInHomes staff may facilitate introductions for premium accounts

**Consumer acquisition:** National marketing, SEO, paid advertising driving families to platform

## Who Can Receive Referrals

- Private-pay home care agencies
- Medicare-certified home health agencies
- Senior living communities

**Services covered:** Personal care, companion care, skilled nursing, specialized care (dementia, Parkinson's, etc.)

## How It Works

1. **Family submits care needs** on CareInHomes.com (location, care type, budget, start date)
2. **Matching algorithm** identifies agencies in service area offering requested services
3. **Lead notification sent to agencies** (email, SMS, portal)
4. **Agencies review lead details** and decide whether to purchase
5. **If purchased:** Agency receives family contact information
6. **Agency follows up** with family to schedule assessment

**Lead pricing:** Varies by exclusivity (shared vs. exclusive) and geography—typically $25-100+ per lead

## How to Get Listed / Enrolled

**Step 1:** Contact CareInHomes
- Call (800) 327-7302
- Email Support@CareInHomes.com
- Visit providers.careinhomes.com

**Step 2:** Create provider account
- Agency information and license
- Geographic service areas
- Services offered and specializations
- Insurance and accreditation

**Step 3:** Set lead preferences
- Define target geographic areas (ZIP codes, cities, radius)
- Select service types you want leads for
- Set budget and lead volume preferences

**Step 4:** Review and purchase leads
- Receive notifications when matching leads available
- Review lead details before purchasing
- Purchase leads that fit your criteria

## Cost and Lead Quality

**Cost:**
- **Per-lead pricing:** Starting ~$25/lead (shared) to $100+ (exclusive)
- **Volume discounts:** Higher commitment = lower per-lead cost
- **No setup fees** (varies by account type)

**Lead Quality:**
- **Qualified:** Families have submitted specific care needs and contact information
- **Immediate need:** Most leads are seeking services within 2-4 weeks
- **Shared vs. exclusive:** Exclusive leads = no competition; shared = 2-5 agencies receive same lead
- **Conversion:** 15-30% for responsive agencies

**Volume:** Scalable—purchase as many or few leads as desired

## Pros and Cons

**Pros:**
- **Qualified leads:** Families actively seeking home care
- **Geographic targeting:** Focus on specific service areas
- **Scalable:** Control lead volume and budget
- **Fast setup:** Quicker than building organic marketing
- **500k monthly visitors:** Large consumer audience

**Cons:**
- **Cost per lead:** $25-100+ adds up quickly
- **Shared leads:** Competition from other agencies (unless paying for exclusivity)
- **Variable conversion:** Not all leads convert to clients
- **Requires fast response:** First agency to contact family often wins
- **CAC must be monitored:** Ensure cost per acquisition < customer lifetime value

## How This Fits Mastering Home Care

**Ingest:** Email notifications or API from CareInHomes provider portal

**Automation:**
- **Lead alerts:** CareInHomes notification → Slack/SMS alert to intake team
- **Auto-populate intake:** Parse CareInHomes lead data → pre-fill intake forms
- **Response tracking:** Monitor time from lead purchase to first family contact
- **Conversion analytics:** Track lead source "CareInHomes" → measure conversion rate and CAC
- **ROI dashboard:** Compare CareInHomes CAC vs. other lead sources

## Best Practices

1. **Respond within 5-15 minutes:** First agency to contact family often wins
2. **Qualify leads before purchasing:** Review lead details—does it match your services and capacity?
3. **Track CAC meticulously:** Calculate cost per acquisition including lead cost + staff time
4. **Test exclusivity:** Compare conversion rates for exclusive vs. shared leads
5. **Set lead volume limits:** Don't over-purchase—ensure you can follow up on all leads
6. **Negotiate volume discounts:** If converting well, commit to higher volume for lower per-lead cost
7. **Build relationships with account managers:** Personal touch can improve lead quality
