1. Only platform admins should be able to invite superadmins. Platform admins should also have all the functionality in the app including adding their own agencies
2. Only superadmins should be able to invite agency admins. Agency admins are invited when the agency is created. 
3. Only agency admins should be able to invite other agency users. The number of users they can invite depends on the package they paid for since pricing is agency sized based

5. Agency admins should also have the ability to upload documents on the user's behalf as well as upload their own documents
6. Document parsing should work as needed to achieve the main goal: credential tracking
7. Inviting admins or users means they receive an email asking them to set their password and log into the platform where they too can upload their own documents for tracking
8. Tracking logic should work correctly
